Coronavirus Facts: There are many different things to know about the current global pandemic, the coronavirus. First, the country with the leading number of cases is currently the United States. Second, people of very young or very old ages are more prone to bad effects from the virus because of a weaker immune system. Lastly, the most important part to flatten the curve is to social distance. This will lower the case numbers and prevent spreading of it.
